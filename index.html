About Me

Hello! I'm Rubén, and I'm a programmer and videogame designer. I love programming and designing video games, bringing the ideas in my head to life.

Additionally, I'm constantly learning new things to both expand my knowledge and gain new experiences. I take my work very seriously and am quite a perfectionist, always prioritizing the delivery of impeccable and optimal work. While I often work alone, some of my best experiences have come from sharing ideas with team members. I not only enjoy bringing my own ideas to life, but I also love working with the ideas of others.

Above all, I strive to grow both professionally and personally.
